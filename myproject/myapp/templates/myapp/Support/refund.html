Refund Policy

We are committed to providing our customers with a smooth and transparent refund process. If you cancel a ticket, the refund will be processed within 7–10 working days to your original payment method.

Please note that cancellation charges will apply depending on the time of cancellation relative to the departure schedule. The following table outlines the applicable charges:

Time Before Departure Refund Percentage Cancellation Charge
More than 48 hours 90% 10%
24–48 hours 75% 25%
12–24 hours 50% 50%
Less than 12 hours 0% 100%

Please ensure that you review the schedule before making a booking to avoid cancellation costs. For assistance, contact our support team.
